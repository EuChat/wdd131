Project Site Plan:Music Festival Extravaganza

Site Name

Music Festival Extravaganza

Reason for the selection: This name encapsulates the vibrant and exciting nature of the music festival.It is memorable and alings well with the brand and identity and purpose of the site.

Site Purpose

The website serves as an interactive platform for the musical festival.It provides details about the event line up, ticket purchasing options and contact information.The site aims to enhance the user exprience by offering a visually appealling and easy to navigate interface.

Scenerios

  • What artists are performing and what time are they scheduled to perform
  • How can I purchase tickets for the festival and what are the different tickets options available

Color Schema

Primary Colors Navy Blue (#1D3557) Used for the background, headers, and navigation bar Golden Yellow (#FFD700) Adds vibrancy and energy, reflecting the excitement and dynamic nature nature of the music festival

Secondary Colors Teal (#008080) Complements the primary colors and provide a modern, refreshing vibe that can appeal to a broad audience. Coral (#FF6F61) Enhances the warm, inviting feel and can be used for call- to- action buttons, banners or highlights.

Accent colors white (#FFFFFF) Ensure readability and keeps the design clean and uncluttered Light grey (#D3D3D3) Useful for secondary information, backgrounds, and sections that will not compete with the main content Electric blue(#7DF9FF) for drawing attention to important elements like ticket purchase buttons, special announcement or key navigation links.

Typography

Font 1 Arial,sans-serif Used for body text and paragraphs Font 2 Georgia,serif Used for headers and titles
